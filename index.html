Our Story

Born in Smoke & Fire

The Grillhouse started in 2010 as a backyard passion project by pitmaster duo Marcus & Leo. Armed with a hand-built offset smoker and family recipes passed down through generations, they turned a weekend hobby into one of the city's most beloved BBQ destinations.

Every cut of meat is sourced from local farms, seasoned with house-made rubs, and smoked low & slow over real hardwood — no shortcuts, no gas, no shortcuts. Just honest fire and the patience it takes to do it right.
